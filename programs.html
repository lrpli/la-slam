Programs that move from page to stage.

Our programs support youth ages 12-21 with creative labs, showcases, and leadership development. Every program is rooted in community care.

After-School Creative Labs

Weekly sessions for writing, performance, and beat-making, led by teaching artists and alumni mentors.

School Partnerships

Residency programs embedded in schools and libraries to bring expressive arts into daily learning.

Citywide Showcases

Quarterly events that celebrate youth voices and connect families, creatives, and local leaders.

Leadership Fellowship

A paid pathway for youth leaders to co-design workshops, mentor peers, and lead community conversations.

Program Outcomes

Improved confidence, public speaking, and writing skills.

Safe spaces for self-expression and peer support.

Pathways to leadership, education, and creative careers.

Bring LA SLAM to your community.

We collaborate with schools, youth programs, and civic partners. Tell us about your community and we will design a program together.

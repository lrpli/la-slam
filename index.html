We champion youth voices through spoken word, music, and community care.

LA SLAM is a Louisiana-based nonprofit focused on empowering young people to create, perform, and lead. We build safe stages, build confidence, and help young artists turn their stories into impact.

What We Deliver

Creative Labs

Weekly labs build writing, rhythm, and stage presence with teaching artists and peer feedback.

Showcase Nights

Quarterly showcases amplify youth stories and celebrate family and community support.

Leadership Pathways

Youth leaders design workshops, mentor peers, and co-produce community events.

Wellness + Belonging

We center mental health, belonging, and accountability through creative care.

Our story is built on community, creativity, and care.

LA SLAM is a nonprofit organization based in Broussard, Louisiana. We cultivate platforms where youth can explore poetry, music, and activism while building lifelong confidence.

Our Mission

Empower Louisiana youth to express their truth through spoken word, music, and community storytelling while nurturing leadership and wellness.

Our Vision

A Louisiana where young creatives feel seen, supported, and equipped to shape a more just, joyful future.

How We Work

Build safe, welcoming spaces led by trusted mentors.

Offer creative tools that help youth process and share their stories.

Partner with schools, libraries, and community centers across Acadiana.

Celebrate youth leadership and support long-term growth.
